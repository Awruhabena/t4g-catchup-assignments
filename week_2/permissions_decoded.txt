Decoding Permissions
a) -rwxr-xr--: Owner can read, write, and execute. Group can read and execute. Others can only read.


b) drwxrwx---: This is a directory. Owner and Group can read, write and execute. Others have no access.


с) -rw-r--r--: Owner can read and write. Group and Others can only read.


d) chmod 750 file: Sets permissions so Owner has full access, Group can read and execute, and Others have no access.


e) chmod 600 file: Sets permissions so Owner can read and write, while Group and Others have no access.
